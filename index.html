CS WITH SANJOY

CS with Sanjoy is a channel for computer science students who are studying BSc, BCA, Btech etc. Sanjoy sir who is B. Sc (Honors), M. Sc (CIS), M. Tech (CSA), B.ED., has more than 15 years of teaching experience and over a thousand students are taught by him. CS with Sanjoy provides free Computer Science Lectures primarily in Bengali for all subjects like Digital Electronics, Data Structures, Algorithm, Programming Languages (C, C++, Python), Database Management System, Theory of Computer Science(Automata), Computer Networks, Computer Architecture, Operating System, Micro Processor , Software Engineering and many more. These lectures are very useful for Computer science students in their university exams and many competitive exams.
